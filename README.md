# 🌊 Cozy Pomodoro & Aesthetic To-Do List
**A minimalist, immersive productivity dashboard featuring ambient soundscapes and integrated task management.**

[Live Demo](https://harsh2981.github.io/Pomodoro-timer/)

## 🚀 Introduction
**Cozy Pomodoro** is a web-based productivity tool designed to help you enter "the flow state" and stay there. By combining the scientifically proven **Pomodoro Technique** with high-definition **ambient soundscapes** (Alpha Waves and White Noise) and **aesthetic static backgrounds**, this app turns work into a peaceful experience.

Whether you are studying for exams, coding your next project, or writing a novel, this tool provides a distraction-free environment to track your time and crush your goals.

## ✨ Key Features
* **Customizable Pomodoro Timer:** Set your own focus and break intervals to match your personal rhythm.
* **Integrated To-Do List:** A slide-out task manager to keep your objectives visible without cluttering the screen.
* **Live Session Tracking:** Real-time statistics showing your total **Focus Time** and **Break Time** per session.
* **Aesthetic Scenes:** High-quality, static nature backgrounds curated to reduce visual fatigue.
* **Focus Audio Player:** Built-in player featuring **Alpha Waves** and **White Noise** for deep concentration.
* **Glassmorphism UI:** A modern, "frosted glass" interface that looks beautiful on any device.

## 🧠 Why Use This Tool?
Using a **Pomodoro Timer** helps prevent burnout by enforcing regular breaks. Combining it with **Alpha Waves** ($8-12 \text{ Hz}$) is known to assist in "wakeful relaxation," making it easier to ignore external distractions and focus on the task at hand.

## 🛠️ Built With
* **HTML5 & CSS3:** For a responsive, hardware-accelerated UI.
* **Vanilla JavaScript:** Lightweight and fast, ensuring zero lag during your focus sessions.
* **GitHub Pages:** Fast, reliable global hosting.

## 📥 Installation & Usage
1.  **Visit the Site:** Simply open the [GitHub Pages link](https://harsh2981.github.io/Pomodoro-timer/).
2.  **Set Your Time:** Use the settings panel to define your focus and break minutes.
3.  **Add Tasks:** Slide out the "Tasks" panel to list your goals.
4.  **Play Audio:** Choose your favorite ambient track and hit start.
